Account Deletion

JewelAI Studio allows users to request account deletion directly from within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the JewelAI Studio app.
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Go to the Profile page.
  4. Tap on Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your request.

What Happens After You Request Deletion

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately.
  • You will no longer be able to access your account.
  • Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Once the deletion process is completed, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User profile information
  • Login/account credentials
  • Uploaded jewelry images
  • AI-generated images and history
  • Credits and purchase-related data linked to the account

Important Notes

  • Deletion requests cannot be undone after confirmation.
  • Data deletion is completed within 30 days from the request date.
  • Some data may be retained if required for legal or regulatory purposes.
